ABOUT THE ACADEMY

HCP Academy is the training and innovation arm of HCP services, established to equip African electricians, technicians and aspiring innovators with the practical skills and knowledge needed to deliver top-tier electrical solutions that meet global standards.

Born from a passion to bridge the skill gap in Africa's technical workforce, HCP Academy launched its flagship program - the ElectroSkills Lab - as a hands on training platforms focused on electrical engineering, embedded systems, circuit design, and smart technologies. Our programs combine real-world projects, industry-relevant content, and expert mentorship to prepare students for both employment and entrepreneurship in the rapidly evolving tech-driven world.

We are committed to empowering thousands of young Africans with cutting-edge skills, fostering a generation of professionals who are not just job-ready but innovation-driven. Whether through in-person workshops or virtual learning environments, HCP Academy is building a community of problem-solvers, creators and leaders capable of transforming Africa's future.

Our Vision is to build a thriving community of young African innovators equipped with technical expertise and mindset to power the continent's future through sustainable solutions.

Our Mission is building a skilled workforce by training 1,000 young people annually in practical electrical and tech-based skills, aiming to reach 5,000+ learners across Africa by 2030 - driving innovation, self-reliance and career opportunities through quality education and guided experience.
